Watch Audio Player

A focused audio playback experience designed for Apple Watch. The app is built around quick access, simple controls, practical file handling, and a compact interface that keeps listening actions easy to reach.

Overview

Watch Audio Player is designed for users who want a direct and lightweight listening experience on Apple Watch. The interface focuses on the core playback moment: seeing what is available, starting audio quickly, and controlling playback without unnecessary screens.

The product direction emphasizes small-screen clarity. Buttons, labels, and file states are organized to reduce friction and make the app feel useful during short interactions.

Core experience

  • Watch-first playback presentation
  • Clear controls for quick interaction
  • Focused file and listening experience
  • Compact layout optimized for glanceable use
  • Simple structure that avoids visual noise

Product goals

The app is shaped as a practical watch utility rather than a complex media library. The main goal is to make local audio access feel immediate, reliable, and understandable from the watch screen.

Release-facing work also includes App Store presentation, icon handling, metadata, and a support path that fits a small but professional utility app.

Technologies

  • SwiftUI
  • watchOS interface design
  • AVFoundation / audio playback workflows
  • File management flows
  • State-driven playback presentation
  • App Store product presentation
