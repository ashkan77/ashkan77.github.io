Overview

BMI Checker Plus is designed to make body mass index checks simple and understandable. The app focuses on a short path from entering height and weight to reading a result without complex screens or distracting content.

The interface is intentionally utility-focused. It presents the calculation clearly, keeps the category readable, and avoids medical claims so the experience stays safe and easy to understand.

Core experience

  • Simple BMI calculation workflow
  • Readable result and category presentation
  • Clean input structure for quick checks
  • Non-diagnostic health copy and user guidance
  • Mobile layout designed for clarity and repetition

Product goals

The app is shaped as a clear health utility: open, enter values, understand the result, and leave with context. It is not designed to replace medical advice, diagnosis, or professional health evaluation.

Design decisions focus on trust, readability, and careful wording. This keeps the product useful for everyday reference while maintaining responsible health communication.

Technologies

  • SwiftUI
  • Deterministic calculation logic
  • State-driven form handling
  • Accessible result presentation
  • Health-safe copy and category display
  • App Store product presentation
