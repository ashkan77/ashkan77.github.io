Overview

World Guide App is built as a structured destination guide. The app organizes countries, cities, attractions, and useful travel information into a mobile experience that is easy to browse before or during a trip.

The product direction emphasizes clarity: users should quickly understand where they are in the content structure, move between destinations, and read attraction information without feeling overwhelmed.

Core experience

  • Country, city, and attraction browsing
  • Organized destination information
  • Travel-focused mobile interface
  • Readable content structure for quick exploration
  • Map and location-oriented destination flows

Product goals

The main goal is to make travel content feel organized and approachable. Instead of presenting a crowded list of unrelated places, the app separates information by destination and keeps each page focused.

Design decisions focus on simple navigation, useful reading hierarchy, and a product structure that can scale as more countries, cities, and categories are added.

Technologies

  • SwiftUI
  • Map and location-oriented product flows
  • Structured local content architecture
  • Reusable destination detail components
  • Offline-friendly content planning
  • App Store product presentation
