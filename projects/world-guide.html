World Guide App

A travel and city guide experience built around curated destinations, in-app exploration, offline-friendly content, accessibility, and clear mobile navigation.

Project Direction

World Guide App presents countries, cities, attractions, local information, and destination details in a structured guide experience. The product direction emphasizes trustworthy content organization, polished mobile UI, and a navigation model that feels clear, fast, and familiar.

Technical Focus

  • SwiftUI-based travel guide interface
  • City, country, and attraction detail pages
  • Offline content structure and curated media planning
  • Map and location-oriented destination flows
  • Accessibility and release-readiness checks

Role

iOS development, content architecture, UI/UX polish, release validation.
