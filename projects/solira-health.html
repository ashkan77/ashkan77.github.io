Solira Health

An Apple-native health companion focused on calm guidance, HealthKit-aware insights, privacy-first architecture, accessibility, widgets, watchOS, and App Store-safe non-diagnostic health language.

Project Direction

Solira Health is designed as a premium health companion that helps users understand patterns in their recent data without replacing clinical judgment. The product direction prioritizes trustworthy summaries, deterministic health logic, clear safety boundaries, and Apple-native interaction patterns.

Technical Focus

  • SwiftUI-first interface architecture
  • HealthKit integration and privacy-first data handling
  • watchOS companion experience
  • WidgetKit, App Intents, and release-readiness flows
  • Accessibility, Dynamic Type, and contrast-focused UI validation

Role

Product design, iOS engineering, UI/UX direction, release hardening.
