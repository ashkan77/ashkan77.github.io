Solira Health

A health companion focused on calm guidance, HealthKit-aware insights, privacy-first architecture, accessibility, widgets, watchOS, and App Store-safe non-diagnostic language.

Project Direction

Solira Health is designed as a premium health companion that helps users understand patterns in recent data without replacing clinical judgment. The product direction prioritizes trustworthy summaries, deterministic health logic, clear safety boundaries, and refined interaction patterns.

Technical Focus

  • SwiftUI-first interface architecture
  • HealthKit integration and privacy-first data handling
  • watchOS companion experience
  • WidgetKit, App Intents, and release-readiness flows
  • Accessibility, Dynamic Type, and contrast-focused UI validation

Role

Product design, iOS engineering, UI/UX direction, release hardening.
