Solira Health Privacy Policy

This page is a public privacy policy placeholder for Solira Health. It should be reviewed and finalized before use in App Store Connect.

Privacy-first approach

Solira Health is intended to respect user privacy and handle health-related information with care. Health data should be used only for user-facing app features and should not be sold to advertisers or data brokers.

Health data

When HealthKit access is requested, users control which categories they share. The app should use granted data only to power health summaries, trends, widgets, and related product features.

Diagnostics and support

Support communication may include information the user chooses to provide by email. This can be used to respond to support requests and improve product quality.
